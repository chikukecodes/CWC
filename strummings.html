🔑 Tips

  • ↓ = Downstroke (towards the floor)
  • ↑ = Upstroke (towards the ceiling)
  • Pause = Rest or muted strum for rhythm
  • Start slow with a metronome and increase gradually
  • Practice with open chords (C, E, G, Dm, D, Em, Am, F7)
